ProITivity - is an online school offering education in programming and data science. Courses are designed to provide students with advanced skills and knowledge relevant to the industry. The curriculum includes a wide range of topics and is taught by experienced faculty using interactive classes, hands-on projects, and real-world case studies. The online format allows the flexibility to study the material at your own pace from anywhere in the world. The school provides a comprehensive curriculum and student support. It is ideal for aspiring and experienced professionals looking to advance their careers.

Data Analytics

The Data Analytics course offers a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical skills. You will work on real business cases and apply your knowledge to various projects. Our experienced instructors will guide you on every step and support your learning efforts.

After completing this course, you will have on-demand data analyst skills to help you become a valuable data analyst and make important fact-based decisions.

Data science DATA SCIENCE

Data Science

The Data Science course offers a hands-on approach where you will apply your knowledge to actual data and solve real-world problems. You will learn to use such software tools and libraries as Python, R, and TensorFlow to work efficiently with data and build models. Our experienced tutors will support you throughout the course and help you overcome challenges.

After completing this course, you will have profound data science skills and be able to apply them in various areas: from prediction and optimization to pattern recognition and text analysis. You will become a sought-after Data Science professional and be able to make meaningful contributions to the world of data and data mining.
